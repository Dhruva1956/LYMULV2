About

Who we are

We offer dance-based fitness classes designed to help you get fit while having fun. We guide you through energetic routines that will leave you feeling energized and motivated.

We are a team of breaking barriers, thinking outside the box and leaving you wanting more. We work to create a 5-6 minute set with a storyline that incorporates a multitude of styles of dance(including Bollywood, South Indian – free style and folk). In our 7 years of remarkable journey, not only captivated audiences but actively promoted the country’s rich culture.

We are “Team Lymul”. – Orgin of Rhythm

What sets us apart is our diverse and inclusive community. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or taking your first steps onto the dance floor, you'll find a supportive and welcoming environment here.

From Latin rhythms to hip-hop grooves, our classes offer something for everyone. Each session is carefully curated to keep you engaged, motivated, and coming back for more. All you need is a passion for movement and a desire to sweat!

We have graced events like IABA and Gujarati Society leaving spectators awestruck with our performances and being continuous winners all these years. Through these efforts, we are not only entertained but also inspired and motivated people to embrace a healthier and fitter lifestyle.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is dance fitness?

    Dance fitness is a fun and dynamic way to exercise that combines traditional fitness movements with dance choreography. It's designed to improve cardiovascular health, strength, flexibility, and coordination while grooving to the rhythm of energetic music.

  • Do I need any dance experience to join your classes?

    Not at all! Our classes are suitable for all levels, from beginners to experienced dancers. Our instructors provide modifications and options to accommodate different skill levels, so you can feel comfortable and confident no matter where you are on your fitness journey.

  • What should I wear and bring to class?

    We recommend wearing comfortable workout attire that allows for freedom of movement, along with supportive athletic shoes. Don't forget to bring a water bottle to stay hydrated and a towel to wipe away any sweat. Optional items may include a yoga mat for floor exercises and any dance accessories you enjoy, like wristbands or dance sneakers.

  • What if I have physical limitations or injuries?

    Safety and inclusivity are our top priorities. Before participating in any activities, please consult with your doctor about any health concerns. If you have any physical limitations or injuries, kindly let us know before the class commences.
